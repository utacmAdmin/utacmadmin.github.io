Our Mission

We aim to further develop, for educational and scientific purposes, the members of this organization in ways including:

  • an increased knowledge of the science, design, development, construction, language, and applications of computing machinery;
  • a greater interest in computing machinery and its applications;
  • and a means of communication between persons having interest in computing machinery
